Company Compliance Information

This portal provides quick access to our IT and security compliance standards. Please refer to your internal documentation for specific guidance or reach out to the compliance team for support.

  • ✔ Encryption: SHA-2, SHA-3 approved. MD5 and SHA-1 not permitted.
  • ✔ Data Retention: Follow IT-80.1 standards.
  • ✔ Access Control: Use multi-factor authentication for all admin accounts.
  • ✔ Logging: Store logs securely for at least 90 days.
